To Sum Me Up

Born and raised in Minnesota, I am the daughter of two Hmong immigrants whose families came to the United States in hopes of better lives. And here I am, a student at a prestigious university--grateful (and privileged) to have grown up with an incredible and supportive family, crazily smart peers, and wonderful teachers. I know, though, that I am part of the lucky few. I aspire to lift people up, bring people together, and make this world more beautiful and wondrous for everyone. There's so much to learn and do, so let's get moving!
